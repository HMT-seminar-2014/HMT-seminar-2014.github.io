Homer Multitext project summer seminar 2014

Repositories from the HMT project's Summer Seminar at the Center for Hellenic Studies

Web site for the seminar: http://www.homermultitext.org/summer2014

Work in these repositories in June, 2014:

  • diplomatic edition of all material (text, scholia, other features) in Iliad 12 in the Venetus A manuscript
  • "The Venetian Homer in 2014": an updated version of T.W. Allen's 19th century article "On the Composition of Some Greek Manuscripts: The Venetian Homer", with URN citation of all evidence
